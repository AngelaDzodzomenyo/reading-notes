# Lists
  ## Ordered 
    * ex. < ol > ordered lists are numbered 
            < li >steak </ li >
            < li >chesse </ li >
            < li > bread </ li >
          </ol >

    > looks like: 
                  1. steak
                  2. cheese
                  3. bread

  ## Unordered list
    * ex. < ul > unordered list are bullet points
            < li >steak </ li >
            < li >chesse </ li >
            < li > bread </ li >
          </ ul >
          
    > looks like : 
                  * steak
                  * cheese
                  * bread
